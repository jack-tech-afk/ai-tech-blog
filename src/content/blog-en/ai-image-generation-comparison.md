## Overview of the Three AI Image Generators

Let's start with a brief overview of each tool.

### What Is Midjourney?

Midjourney is an AI image generator developed by an independent AI lab. It grew primarily through its Discord platform and has earned massive support from creators thanks to its outstanding image quality and artistic expression. A web-based interface has also been released, making it more accessible than ever.

### What Is DALL-E 3?

DALL-E 3 is an AI image generator developed by OpenAI, integrated into ChatGPT. It's known for generating images that faithfully follow natural language instructions and excels at creating images that contain text. It's available at no additional cost for ChatGPT Plus subscribers.

### What Is Stable Diffusion?

Stable Diffusion is an open-source AI image generator developed by Stability AI. It can be installed and run on your own PC, making it ideal for privacy-conscious users or those who need to generate large volumes of images. With custom models and LoRA extensions, it offers the highest level of customizability.

## Image Quality Comparison: Which Looks Best?

### Midjourney's Image Quality

Midjourney delivers **top-tier image quality** among AI image generators. It excels particularly in these areas:

- **Photorealistic** — Images that look like actual photographs
- **Art & Illustration** — Painterly and concept art-style expressions
- **Fantasy & Sci-Fi** — Dramatic and fantastical scenes
- **Architecture & Interior** — Architectural renders and interior designs

Midjourney images are "beautiful by default." Even with short prompts, the composition, lighting, and color balance are well-polished.

### DALL-E 3's Image Quality

DALL-E 3 stands out for its **faithfulness to prompts**. It particularly excels at:

- **Text-embedded images** — Posters, logos, and images containing text
- **Conceptual imagery** — Strong ability to visualize abstract concepts
- **Precise compositions** — Generating images that closely match detailed instructions

However, in terms of photorealism and artistic beauty, it falls slightly behind Midjourney.

### Stable Diffusion's Image Quality

Stable Diffusion's quality **varies significantly depending on the base model and customization**.

- **SDXL** and **SD3** models deliver high-quality images
- **Custom models (checkpoints)** enable style-specific, high-quality output
- **LoRA** and other extensions can further enhance quality

However, achieving top-quality results requires prompt engineering skills and model selection knowledge, creating a higher barrier for beginners.

## Ease of Use Comparison

### Midjourney: Discord-Based but Intuitive

Midjourney primarily operates through Discord. Simply type a prompt in a text channel and images are generated — the operation itself is straightforward.

**Basic Workflow:**
1. Join the Midjourney Discord server
2. Enter the `/imagine` command in a text channel
3. Describe the image you want in English
4. Four images are generated in seconds to tens of seconds
5. Select your favorite and upscale it

A web version is also available for a more intuitive browser-based experience.

**Key Points:**
- Requires familiarity with Discord
- English prompts recommended
- Fine-tuning via parameters (`--ar`, `--style`, etc.)
- Web version is beginner-friendly

### DALL-E 3: The Most Beginner-Friendly Option

DALL-E 3 is **integrated into ChatGPT**, so if you're already comfortable with ChatGPT, you can start using it immediately with zero learning curve.

**Basic Workflow:**
1. Access ChatGPT (Plus or higher)
2. Ask for an image in natural language
3. Image is generated in seconds
4. Refine through conversational instructions

**Key Points:**
- Supports multilingual prompts
- Conversational refinement is possible
- No separate tool or app needed
- Works without any image generation knowledge

DALL-E 3 is the most recommended tool for first-time AI image generation users.

### Stable Diffusion: Advanced Users Only, but Maximum Freedom

Stable Diffusion is typically **installed on a local PC**. While setup requires technical knowledge, it offers the most flexible environment available.

**Basic Workflow (Local):**
1. Prepare a PC with a compatible GPU
2. Install a WebUI (Automatic1111 or ComfyUI)
3. Download model files
4. Access the WebUI via browser and enter prompts
5. Adjust various parameters and generate images

**Key Points:**
- High initial setup barrier
- Requires a high-performance GPU (8GB+ VRAM recommended)
- Most flexible image generation when mastered
- Also available via cloud services (Google Colab, etc.)

Best suited for technically inclined users or those needing bulk image generation.

## Prompt Writing Tips

The quality of AI-generated images largely depends on **how you write your prompts**.

### Basic Structure

Effective prompts should include these elements:

1. **Subject** — What to depict (e.g., a samurai warrior)
2. **Style** — What art style (e.g., oil painting, anime style)
3. **Setting** — The environment (e.g., in a misty forest)
4. **Lighting** — Light effects (e.g., golden hour, dramatic lighting)
5. **Composition** — Camera angle and framing (e.g., close-up, bird's eye view)
6. **Quality** — Quality specifications (e.g., highly detailed, 8K)

### Tool-Specific Tips

**Midjourney:**
- English prompts recommended
- Leverage parameters (`--ar 16:9`, `--style raw`, etc.)
- Even simple prompts produce high-quality results

**DALL-E 3:**
- Supports multilingual prompts
- Conversational instructions work well ("Make it a bit brighter")
- More detailed descriptions yield more faithful images

**Stable Diffusion:**
- Use positive/negative prompts strategically
- Weight keywords with `(keyword:1.5)` for emphasis
- Optimize prompts for the specific model being used

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q: Which is best for complete beginners?

**A:** DALL-E 3. It's integrated into ChatGPT, so you can generate images by simply describing what you want in natural language. Conversational refinement makes it the ideal entry point for AI image generation.

### Q: Are there free AI image generators?

**A:** Stable Diffusion is open source and free to use, but requires a capable GPU. DALL-E 3 is partially available through free ChatGPT, but with generation limits.

### Q: Who owns the copyright to AI-generated images?

**A:** Legal frameworks around AI-generated image copyright are still evolving worldwide. Generally, AI-generated images tend not to be recognized as copyrightable works, though interpretations vary by country. For commercial use, review each tool's terms of service and consult legal experts when needed.

### Q: Can I generate images of real people?

**A:** Most AI image generators restrict the generation of images resembling real people in their terms of service. To avoid ethical and legal risks, we recommend against generating images of real individuals.

### Q: Can I use AI image generators on my phone?

**A:** DALL-E 3 is available through the ChatGPT mobile app. Midjourney can be accessed via mobile browsers and apps. Stable Diffusion is primarily for local use but can be accessed via cloud services on mobile devices.
