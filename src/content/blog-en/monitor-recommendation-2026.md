## PC Monitor Basics: What You Need to Know

Let's start with four essential specs to understand before choosing a monitor.

### Resolution

Resolution indicates how many pixels are packed into the screen. Higher numbers mean sharper images and crisper text.

| Resolution | Name | Pixel Count | Best For |
|:---|:---:|:---:|:---|
| 1920×1080 | Full HD | ~2.07M | Budget-friendly, entry-level gaming |
| 2560×1440 | WQHD | ~3.69M | Balance of gaming and productivity |
| 3840×2160 | 4K UHD | ~8.29M | Remote work, creative work |
| 3440×1440 | UWQHD | ~4.96M | Ultrawide, multitasking |

**For 27-inch or larger monitors, 4K is recommended.** At Full HD, text can appear blurry, causing eye strain during long work sessions. For 24-inch or smaller screens, Full HD or WQHD displays look perfectly sharp.

### Panel Types (IPS / VA / TN)

The panel type significantly affects display quality. Understand each type's strengths to match your needs.

| Panel | Viewing Angle | Contrast | Response Time | Color Accuracy | Best For |
|:---|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---|
| **IPS** | **Wide (178°)** | Average | Slightly slow | **Excellent** | Remote work, creative |
| **VA** | Moderately wide | **High** | Average | Good | Movies, dark rooms |
| **TN** | Narrow | Low | **Fastest** | Below average | Competitive gaming |

The 2026 trend favors **IPS (or Nano IPS) panels**. Their wide viewing angles and superior color accuracy make them the top choice for virtually all use cases. VA panels excel at contrast, making them ideal for movie watching and dark-theme workflows.

### Refresh Rate

Refresh rate indicates how many times per second the screen updates (measured in Hz).

| Refresh Rate | Experience | Best For |
|:---|:---|:---|
| 60Hz | Standard smoothness | Office work, browsing |
| 75Hz | Slightly smoother | Remote work (budget pick) |
| 144Hz | **Noticeably smoother** | Casual gaming |
| 165–240Hz | Very smooth | Competitive FPS, fighting games |
| 360Hz+ | Pro-level | Esports athletes |

For primarily remote work, **60–75Hz is sufficient**. If you also game, **144Hz or higher** eliminates screen stuttering for a much smoother experience.

### Choosing the Right Size

Match the size to your desk depth and intended use.

| Size | Recommended Desk Depth | Notes |
|:---|:---:|:---|
| 24 inches | 50cm+ | Compact. Good as a secondary display |
| 27 inches | 60cm+ | **Most versatile. The standard main monitor** |
| 32 inches | 70cm+ | Large, immersive. Pairs perfectly with 4K |
| 34-inch UW | 70cm+ | Ultrawide. Side-by-side windows with ease |

When in doubt, **27 inches is the safest choice**. If you have a spacious desk, consider 32 inches or a 34-inch ultrawide.
