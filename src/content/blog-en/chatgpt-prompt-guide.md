## The 5 Fundamental Rules of Effective Prompts

Before diving into specific techniques, let's cover the **five core principles** for writing effective prompts.

### Rule 1: Assign a Role (Role-Playing)

By telling ChatGPT "You are a [role]," you can elicit responses as if from an expert in that field.

**Bad example:** Tell me how to write an email.

**Good example:** You are a business etiquette expert. Please teach me how to write an apology email to a client.

### Rule 2: Provide Specific Conditions

Instead of vague instructions, specify **word count, format, target audience, and tone**.

**Bad example:** Write a blog article.

**Good example:** Write a blog article with the following specifications. Topic: Improving remote work productivity. Word count: approximately 2,000 words. Target audience: professionals in their 30s. Tone: casual yet trustworthy.

### Rule 3: Specify the Output Format

By clearly indicating the **desired output format** — tables, bullet points, step-by-step instructions, etc. — you'll get more usable responses.

### Rule 4: State Constraints Explicitly

Adding **constraints** like "Do not include X" or "Within the scope of Y" helps eliminate unnecessary information.

### Rule 5: Give Instructions Step by Step

For complex tasks, break them into **stages** rather than giving everything at once. This improves accuracy.

## Business Email Techniques (5 Prompts)

### Technique 1: Apology Email to a Client

```
You are a business etiquette expert. Please draft an apology email to a client for the following situation.

[Situation]
- Original delivery date: March 15
- Actual delivery date: March 22 (1 week delay)
- Reason for delay: Parts procurement delays
- Preventive measures: Securing alternative suppliers

[Requirements]
- Polite and sincere tone
- Include specific measures to prevent recurrence
- Include a subject line
```

### Technique 2: Proposal Email

```
You are a sales professional. Please draft an email proposing the following service to a prospective customer.

[Service Details] (Enter service description here)
[Customer Pain Points] (Enter expected challenges here)
[Email Tone] Polite but not pushy
[Goal] Secure a free consultation appointment
```

### Technique 3: Meeting Minutes Email

```
Please create a meeting minutes email for stakeholders based on the following meeting notes.

[Meeting Notes]
(Paste your bullet-point notes here)

[Format]
- Subject line
- Attendees
- Decisions made (bullet points)
- Action items (with assignees and deadlines)
- Next meeting date
```

### Technique 4: Polite Decline Email

```
Please draft a polite business email declining the following request.

[Request to Decline] (Description of the request)
[Reason] (Honest reason)
[Requirements]
- Maintain the relationship
- Mention alternatives or future possibilities
- Express gratitude
```

### Technique 5: English Business Email

```
Please convert the following content into a professional English business email.

[Content] (Enter your message in your preferred language)

[Requirements]
- Formal business English
- Concise and clear expressions
- Include a subject line in English
- Include a proper closing
```

## Advanced Techniques for Power Users

### Chain Prompting (Conversation Chaining)

A technique for **giving instructions in stages** within a single conversation. Start with the big picture, refine the details, then polish — this workflow produces high-quality outputs.

**Example:**
1. "Create an outline for a proposal about X."
2. "Make the second section more specific."
3. "Review the entire document for consistency and suggest improvements."

### Few-Shot Prompting (Providing Examples)

A technique where you **show ChatGPT 1-3 examples of ideal output** before giving your instruction. This effectively communicates the desired format and quality level.

### Negative Prompting (Specifying What NOT to Do)

A technique that **explicitly states what to avoid**. Useful when you want to eliminate unnecessary preambles or disclaimers.

**Example:** "Skip the introduction and disclaimers. Provide only the answer."

### Meta Prompting (Getting the AI to Write Prompts)

An advanced technique where you **ask ChatGPT to generate effective prompts** for you.

**Example:** "Create 5 optimal prompts to achieve the following goal: [your goal]"
